Who are We?

The NGO has been working for education, rehabilitation and uplift of the underprivileged sections of the NGO by providing opportunities for education, by imparting employment oriented vocational skills and distributing articles of basic needs to the needy.

Our Origin

Shubhkakshika Educational Society was formes and registered in June 1993 under Society Act 1860 by professors of Delhi University, school teachers, Professionals and others committed to social causes.

Vision and Mission

Upliftment and Elevation of underprivileged and deprived section of society,
To contribute towards educated and empowered India,
To rehabilitate the marginalised sections socially and economically,
Love and care for Every child.
